If you have debt, you need to hear this.
Minimum payments are a math trap.
Banks engineer them to keep you paying interest forever.
Drop five thousand on a standard card. Pay only the minimum.
You will be making payments for over twenty years.
You will pay almost double in interest alone.
The system wants you comfortable. Not debt-free.
Break the cycle today.
Keep the minimum on autopay. Never miss it.
Then attack the rest.
Pick one balance. Funnel every spare dollar straight to the principal.
Skip the takeout. Cancel unused subs. Redirect that cash.
Watch the number drop.
Interest shrinks. Progress speeds up.
