If you have debt, you need to hear this.

You are paying it wrong.

Two methods actually work. Snowball. And avalanche.

Snowball means you list debts smallest to largest. Pay minimums on the rest. Throw every spare dollar at the smallest balance. Knock it out. Roll that payment to the next one.

Quick wins build momentum. It is pure psychology.

Avalanche means you list debts highest interest to lowest. Attack the most expensive one first. Saves thousands. But it takes longer to see a zero balance.

So which do you pick?

Struggle to stay motivated? Take the snowball. You need early wins to keep going.

Love math and want maximum savings? Take the avalanche.

Both destroy debt if you stay consistent.
